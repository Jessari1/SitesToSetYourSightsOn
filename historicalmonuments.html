Statue of Liberty

Things To do

At the Statue of Liberty one of the things to do is climb 146 steps to the crown but you have to pay for a limited ticket for the privelage. A cheaper ticket is required to view the grounds and see the beautifil view of the water around the statue. On the second floor there is a Exhibit with photos and artifacts which tell the story of the statue's creation

How To Get Here

You have to buy a ticket and take a ferry that leaves from either battery park or liberty state park in New Jersey.
