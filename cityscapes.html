Times Square

Things To Do

There are so many different things to do in Times Square, I don't even know where to start. There are many resturants around times sqaure like T.G.I Fridays, Applebees, and Dallas BBQ to name a few. Shopping is frequent in times square due to the vast amount of stores with in walking distance. Watching movies and plays are also nice ways to kill time.

Fun Facts

  • The location is not a square, but Times Square got it's name when the NY Times moved their HQ there
